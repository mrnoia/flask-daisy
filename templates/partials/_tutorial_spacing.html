5. Spacing & Padding

Control margins, padding, and gaps between elements

Padding (p-*)

Space inside an element

p-2 (padding: 0.5rem)
p-4 (padding: 1rem)
p-8 (padding: 2rem)
<div class="p-4">
  Padding on all sides
</div>

<div class="px-4 py-8">
  Horizontal padding 4, vertical padding 8
</div>
Padding variations: p-* (all), px-* (horizontal), py-* (vertical), pt-* (top), pb-* (bottom), pl-* (left), pr-* (right)

Margin (m-*)

Space outside an element

m-2 (margin: 0.5rem)
m-4 (margin: 1rem)
<div class="m-4">
  Margin on all sides
</div>

<div class="mx-auto">
  Center with auto margins
</div>
